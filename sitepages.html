I created Trust Balancer as an excercise in branding and responsive design. This site works on both desktop and mobile devices without redirecting the user by using media queries. The site collapses the top menu options to a slide-in menu on mobile devices or when the width of the browser is too narrow. For the brand of Trust Balancer, I learned of the company through a friend and wanted to rebrand it to look more corporate and powerful to connote trustworthiness to the customer. The image used in the background also dynamically fills the background with correct proportions and is free to use under Creative Commons. All links redirect here.
