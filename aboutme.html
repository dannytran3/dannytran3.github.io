    About Me

    I strive to constantly be learning new things. Currently I am learning JavaScript in terms of web development, AJAX and JQuery. I have experience in Java and JavaScript, mainly using JavaScript in a game development environment. My interest in web technology started last year and has been growing since. My goal is to become a full stack web developer.

    On Design

    "Nuff Said"... a catchphrase of comic legend Stan Lee and a philosophy I take into my work. I aspire to a great designer. Whether it be in games, web, or print, I believe good design is simple design. Good design to me, lets the content be the focus with extreme clarity. It grabs its audience and makes them more willing to accept the message of the content.
